About Me

Hello! I am a PhD Research Scholar in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at IIT Bombay, where I work under the guidance of Prof. Manas Thakur.

I am a member of the CompL group within the PLATO Lab, focusing on compiler optimizations and program analysis. My research centers on developing speculative static and dynamic program analysis techniques for Java’s JIT compiler, with the goal of enhancing runtime performance through advanced optimization strategies.
